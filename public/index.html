Assistive Technology Hub

At Dell Technologies, we want our Team Members to feel empowered in their workspace and equipeed for success.All Team Members have access to emerging assistive technologies to help guarantee their productivity at work. These assistive technologies include built-in tools that are already on your Dell laptop as well as other tools that can be ordered or installed. To find assistive technology that fits your needs, take any (or all) of the three quizzes below and learn more about how Dell can work for you.

Vision

Includes all varieties of vision disabilities or impairments, such as color blindness or corneal graft.

Hearing

Includes complete or partial deafness, as well as issues related to translation.

Cognitive/Psychological

Includes any kind of impairment to learning or productivity, including dyslexia and speech disorders.
If you feel that your needs are not represented in any of these categories, or if there is another tool that you would prefer to use that is not currently approved by Dell Technologies, please fill out this form and the Team Member Experience group will address your request promptly.
